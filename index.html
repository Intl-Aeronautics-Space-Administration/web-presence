Homepage

This is the homepage for the International Aeronautics and Space Administration. The creation of this organization is the result of the "ISAU" proposal which describes why society needs a regulatory authority for interstellar travel and transport. This organization is responsible for the oversight of regulating spaceflight to make sure Earth does not end up trapped inside a shield of space rubble and sattelites travelling at thousands of kilometres per hour making it effectively impossible to launch anything into space.
